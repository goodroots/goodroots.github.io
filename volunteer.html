Come grow with us!


The farm is supported by devoted volunteers from the community or those who come to experience the peaceful environment of the monastery and to be of help on the farm.

VISIT VOLUNTEER

Volunteer Days

There are many ways to volunteer at Good Roots Farm, ranging from hands-on gardening to helping in the greenhouse and composting. Gardening experience is a bonus but not required.

You can join our existing work party of energetic, fun and dedicated volunteers on Friday and Saturday mornings from 8:00-11am or volunteer on Sunday afternoon from 1:00pm to 4:00pm.
